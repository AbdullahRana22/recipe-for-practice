Coconut Cake Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Coconut
  • 3 Egg
  • 1 Teaspoon Baking Powder
  • 1 Cup Coconut Milk
  • 1 Cup All Purpose Flour
  • 1\2 Caster Sugar
  • 1\2 Unsalted Butter

    • Method

      STEP:1

      Whisk Sugar And Butter

      To begin with this delicious cake, whisk butter and sugar till it
      turns fluffy. Then crack open the eggs and whisk the batter till it turns foamy.

      STEP:2

      Prepare The Batter

      Add flour, baking powder and coconut milk and mix until they mix well. Carefully add whole milk in
      small amounts. Lastly, add in the grated coconut and mix the batter well.

      STEP:3

      Bake The Cake

      Preheat the oven to 176-degree Celsius. Take a baking tray and grease it with the remaining butter. Pour the mixture into the tray and bake it
      for around 20-30 minutes. Check before removing from the oven.
